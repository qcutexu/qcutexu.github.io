Hello! I obtained his Ph.D. in Computational Analysis and Modeling in 2014 from Louisiana Tech University. He received postdoc training at the University of Houston and the University of California, Berkeley during 2014-2020. Before joining TTU, he was a visiting assistant professor in the Department of Mathematics at the University of California, Irvine. His research focuses on numerical solutions to fluid-structure interaction problems, emphasizing biomedical-related applications. His expertise lies in computational mathematics, parallel computing, and machine learning.
